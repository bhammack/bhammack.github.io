Pan-seared Ribeye Steak

Jul 7, 2020 in RECIPES
1 min read

Ingredients

1 - 1&1/4 " thick. Weighted pan, set heat to 7 (HIGH). Pan may smoke before steak is on, that is ok.

Steps

  1. Season steak in S&P (and probably butter beforehand). Butter will burn in pan if thrown in, so don’t.
  2. Place in pan. Not too much splattering. For medium/med-rare, cook 4-5 minutes per side.
  3. Noteworthy, when blood begins to seep out the top of the steak, it is getting close to done.
