Oven-baked Chicken Breasts

Mar 21, 2020 in RECIPES
1 min read

Ingredients

  • 1 - 1.5 pounds boneless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Wash chicken breasts, coat in olive oil and italian seasonings.
  3. Lightly grease glass baking pan. place breasts in pan, and garnish with black pepper
  4. Cook 24 minutes.
