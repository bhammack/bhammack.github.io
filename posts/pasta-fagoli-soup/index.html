Pasta Fagoli Soup

Feb 14, 2020 in RECIPES
1 min read

Ingredients

  • 3 slices salt pork (or half pack bacon)
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp roasted garlic
  • 10oz frozen seasoning blend (onions, peppers, celery)
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 32oz reduced-sodium chicken soup broth
  • 2 cans great northern beans
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomato pesto important!
  • 1 cup ditalini pasta

Steps

  1. Cut salt pork into small pieces. Either cook in microwave or directly into a large saucepan. Add olive oil to cook.
  2. Stir in garlic and frozen seasoning blend; cook 4 minutes or until browned. Stir often
  3. Stir in remaining ingredients except pasta. Bring to boil.
  4. Stir in pasta, cover and return to rolling boil for 8-10 minutes or until pasta is tender.
